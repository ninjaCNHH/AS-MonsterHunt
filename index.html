Anson's Monster Hunt

This is Anson's Black Belt project from Codeninjas Hacienda Heights

Go on a quest, choose to defeat or capture Monster's and slay the evil Wizard!

How To Play

  1. Full screen is highly recommended
  2. Use A and D to move left/right in the overworld
  3. Choose to defeat enemies by fighting, or capture them to add to your team.
